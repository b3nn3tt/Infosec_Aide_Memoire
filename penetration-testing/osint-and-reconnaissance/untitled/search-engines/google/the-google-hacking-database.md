# The Google Hacking Database

As part of Offensive Security's [ExploitDB](https://www.exploit-db.com/), there is the [Google Hacking Database](https://www.exploit-db.com/google-hacking-database) \(GHDB\); a resource that contains thousands of useful Google Dork Examples:

![The GHDB interface](../../../../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%28103%29.png)

Simply search for useful Dorks, and GHDB will make suggestions based on your parameters.

Lovely stuff!
