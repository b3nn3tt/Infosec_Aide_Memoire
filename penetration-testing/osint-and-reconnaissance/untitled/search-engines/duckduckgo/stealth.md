# Stealth

A **REALLY** cool operator for DuckDuckGo is the bang \(`!`\) symbol, which allows you to outsource your search to another search engine \(so you don't have to touch it directly - we're looking at you here, Google!\).

Go to the search bar, and hit ! to see the various options available:

![Lot&apos;s of options to outsource your search to](../../../../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%2888%29.png)

So, we will perform a search and outsource it to Google:

![](../../../../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%28133%29.png)
