# Search Engines

A natural place to look is via an online search engine. [Google](https://www.google.co.uk) is no doubt the market leader, but you should also look at other engines, such as [bing](https://www.bing.com/), [duckduckgo](https://duckduckgo.com/) and others, such as the somewhat controversial [Shodan](https://www.shodan.io/).

When you use a search engine, you are not actively scouring the open internet. You are merely searching the database of that particular search engine to look at resources they have discovered whilst they scour the internet themselves. Consequently, each search engine could well produce wildly different results, depending on the thing you are looking for.
