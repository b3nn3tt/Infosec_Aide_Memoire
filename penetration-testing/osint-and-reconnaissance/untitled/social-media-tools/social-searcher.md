# Social Searcher

[Social-Searcher](https://www.social-searcher.com/) is a search engine for social media sites. A free account will allow a limited number of searches per day, but you can pay a fee to lift those restrictions. Social-searcher can be a quick alternative to setting up API keys on multiple more specialized services \(one of the main gripes with Recon-NG\):

![Typical Social-Searcher results](../../../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%2877%29.png)

The search results will include information posted by your target, and what people are saying about them. Among other things, this can help us determine what sort of footprint and coverage an organization has on social media. Once we’ve done this, we may choose to move on to using site-specific tools.
